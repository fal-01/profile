Experience

As a composer of music for multiple indie short films, including an award-winning film from the Las Vegas and Berlin film festivals, I demonstrated my ability to compose music to strict deadlines and to follow the brief set by the Directors. I also worked as a foley operator and did the sound mastering on the films I worked on. I was commissioned to write the music for several episodes of ITV’s popular soap operas, Coronation Street and Emmerdale. I worked to strict deadlines, followed the brief set by the Directors and produced music that was of the quality required for national television.

Other Work

As producer, director, and composer of a musical show that had a two-week run in a theatre on “The Mile” at the Edinburgh Fringe, I was responsible for financing and budgeting the production, casting, employing musicians, organising accommodation and travel requirements, scheduling rehearsals, technical and sound liaison, and publicity.
